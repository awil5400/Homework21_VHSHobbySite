Gems

In the VHS world, tapes that are rare and/or highly sought after are referred to as "gems". These tapes can range from many eras and genres, so knowing what's worth collecting can be overwhelming for newbies. Here, we catalogue some notable tapes that any collector would be happy to add to their stack.
